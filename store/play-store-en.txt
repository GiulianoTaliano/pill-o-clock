💊 Pill O-Clock — The medication alarm that actually rings

Never miss a dose again. Pill O-Clock is a complete personal health assistant: it fires a real alarm sound when it's time to take your medication, tracks every dose you take (or skip), monitors your health measurements with trend charts, and records how you feel each day — all stored privately on your phone. No account. No subscription. No data leaving your device. Ever.

▸ ALARMS THAT ACTUALLY RING
Most reminder apps send a silent notification that's easy to ignore. Pill O-Clock rings a full alarm sound that repeats every 5 minutes until you respond. Take the dose, snooze for 15 minutes, or skip — all directly from the notification or lock screen, without opening the app.

▸ COMPLETE MEDICATION MANAGEMENT
Add any medication with its name, dose (mg, ml, drops, tablets, capsules…), category, special instructions, color tag, treatment period, and an optional photo of the packaging. Set one or multiple alarms per day, only on certain days of the week, or every single day — or mark it as on-demand (PRN) for unscheduled doses like pain relievers or antihistamines. Track your current stock and get automatic low-stock alerts before you run out.

▸ HEALTH MEASUREMENTS
Record blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), blood glucose, weight, SpO₂, and heart rate. Each metric shows your latest value, a sparkline preview, and a full trend chart so you can easily spot patterns over time. Set an optional daily reminder to measure.

▸ DAILY WELLBEING DIARY
Log your mood on a 5-level scale and track symptoms every day — headache, nausea, fatigue, dizziness, stomach pain, anxiety, insomnia, and more. Past entries are editable at any time. Correlate how you feel with your medication history at a glance.

▸ PDF HEALTH REPORT FOR YOUR DOCTOR
Generate a comprehensive health report as a PDF in one tap — active medications, 30-day dose history, health measurements, and diary entries — ready to share with your doctor or specialist at your next appointment.

▸ HOME SCREEN WIDGET (ANDROID)
Place a 2×1 widget on your home screen to see your next pending dose and mark it as taken — without opening the app.

▸ ADHERENCE STREAK & HISTORY
A flame chip on the home screen shows your consecutive days of full adherence. The History tab shows taken, skipped, and missed doses with a clear adherence rate percentage. Switch to the monthly view for a color-coded adherence heatmap — green, yellow, and red days — to spot patterns instantly.

▸ VISUAL CALENDAR
Monthly view that groups your doses by day and status — pending, taken, skipped, and missed — so you can instantly spot days with issues and stay on track.

▸ MEDICAL APPOINTMENTS
Keep all your upcoming and past medical appointments organized: doctor name, location with map pin, date/time, notes, and a configurable reminder (1 hour, 2 hours, or 1 day before).

▸ YOUR DATA IS YOURS — ALWAYS
Everything is stored in a private local database on your device. Nothing is ever sent to external servers. No account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads. Export a full backup (medications, history, appointments, health data, and diary) at any time and restore it if you switch phones.

▸ SPANISH & ENGLISH
The app automatically detects your device language. Spanish and English are both fully supported, and you can switch languages manually from Settings at any time.

▸ DARK MODE & CLEAN DESIGN
Follows your system's light/dark theme automatically. Clean, readable typography and color-coded status indicators make it easy to use at a glance.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Pill O-Clock is a reminder and tracking tool and does not replace the advice of a qualified healthcare professional. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist before changing your medication regimen.
