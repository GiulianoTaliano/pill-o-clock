💊 Pill-O-Clock — Never miss a dose again

Pill-O-Clock is your personal medication assistant. It reminds you exactly when to take each medication, rings an alarm with quick-action buttons, logs your adherence, tracks your health measurements with trends, and lets you record how you feel every day — all stored privately on your device.

▸ ALARMS THAT ACTUALLY RING
Reminders with a real alarm sound that repeat every 5 minutes until you respond. Mark the dose as taken, snooze it 15 minutes, or skip it — all without opening the app, directly from the notification.

▸ COMPLETE MEDICATION MANAGEMENT
Organize all your medications with name, dose (mg, ml, drops, tablets, capsules...), category, instructions, and color. Set daily alarms or only on specific days of the week. Define a treatment period with start and end dates. Track stock and get low-stock alerts.

▸ HEALTH MEASUREMENTS
Record blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), blood glucose, weight, SpO₂, and heart rate. Each metric shows your latest value, a sparkline, and a full trend chart so you can spot patterns over time. Set an optional daily reminder to measure.

▸ DAILY WELLBEING DIARY
Log your mood (5 levels) and symptoms every day — headache, nausea, fatigue, dizziness, and more. Editable at any time. Correlate how you feel with your medication history at a glance.

▸ PDF HEALTH REPORT
Generate and share a comprehensive report as a PDF — medications, dose history, health measurements, and diary entries — ready to bring to your doctor appointment.

▸ ADHERENCE TRACKING
The History tab shows which doses you took, skipped, or missed over the last 30 days. Your adherence rate gives you a clear picture of how well you are following your treatment.

▸ VISUAL CALENDAR
Monthly view that groups your doses by day and status — pending, taken, skipped, and missed — so you can quickly spot days with issues.

▸ YOUR DATA BELONGS TO YOU
Everything is saved on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers. You can export a full backup (medications + history) at any time and restore it if you switch phones.

▸ MEDICAL APPOINTMENTS
Keep track of upcoming and past appointments with doctor, location, date/time, notes, and a configurable reminder.

▸ SPANISH & ENGLISH
The app automatically detects your device's language and also lets you change it manually from Settings.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Pill-O-Clock is a reminder and tracking tool and does not replace the advice of a qualified healthcare professional. Always consult your doctor or pharmacist.
